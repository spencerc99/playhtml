The satchel + bottles (live)

These boot automatically via the extension's real initGlobalFeatures:

  • Inventory satchel — the backpack edge nub on the right. Drag it, click to open the cozy kit, click an item to arm it (it rides next to your cursor). Press Esc to disarm.
  • Bottles — a message bottle places itself somewhere on this page (it's a real synced experiment — open it, read/add, seal).
  • Scissors — open the satchel and arm the scissors, then drag a line across an element to tear it open. Cuts persist locally across refreshes. Press Delete, Backspace, or Cmd/Ctrl+Z while armed to undo the latest cut.
  • Hammer — arm it and click an element repeatedly to dent, displace, and crack it. Impacts persist locally; the same undo shortcuts remove the latest hit while the hammer is armed.
  • Emote wheel — press Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+E to open a radial wheel of emotes at your cursor. Click one or press its number key (1–0) to play it on your cursor; it broadcasts to anyone else on this page. Esc closes the wheel. Interaction emotes (heart, high five, nuzzle, poke) aim at the nearest cursor.
